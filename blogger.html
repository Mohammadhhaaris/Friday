Our Mission

This Blog is about introducing the real Afghanistan and the beauty inside it, World known Afghanistan as a terrorist country, violence, corruption and poor. Yet the reality had been hidden for a while, such as there is alwasy a bright side to everything, same with Afghanistan. Our goal is to show the bright side, the beauty of Afghanistan to the world, specially the inspiring culture of hospitality in Afghanistan, the historical place, the hoobies, education and economic.
